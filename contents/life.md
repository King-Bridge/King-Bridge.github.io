> "Robotics is the pursuit of precision in the physical world, while music is the pursuit of resonance in the spiritual one. For me, the clarinet is not just an instrument, but a medium to explore the balance between discipline and expression."

I am honored to serve as the **Principal Clarinetist** of the **Tsinghua University Symphony Orchestra (TUSO)**. My journey in music has been a parallel path to my research, teaching me the importance of collaboration, listening, and the relentless pursuit of perfection.
